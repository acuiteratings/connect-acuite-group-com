By accessing or using Acuité Connect, each employee or authorised user agrees to these Terms & Conditions. Connect is an internal, workplace-safe, social and communication platform. It is meant to support engagement and collaboration, but it does not replace formal management processes, HR processes, approval workflows, expense policies, employee handbooks, compensation structures, or any other official company policy or contract.

Posts, notices, listings, comments, approvals, invitations, recognitions, Acuite Coin balances, store requests, library activity, leadership interactions, or any other feature on Connect are operational or engagement tools only. Admins and authorised reviewers act on a best-effort basis. Users must keep all content professional, lawful, respectful, accurate, and suitable for an internal workplace platform.
