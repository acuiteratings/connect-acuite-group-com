Business & Finance Quiz

A multiplayer business and finance quiz for up to 4 people. Ten timed questions. Highest score wins.

Multiplayer quiz

Lobby

Invite up to 3 colleagues, choose the difficulty, and answer each business or finance question within 15 seconds.

Rules

Quick rules

  • 2 to 4 players can join one quiz.
  • The host can invite up to 3 colleagues.
  • The host chooses the difficulty.
  • 10 questions are chosen randomly for each match.
  • Each question has 15 seconds.
  • There is no negative marking.
  • The highest score wins.

Battleship

A restrained, turn-based 2-player game for off-peak hours inside Connect.

Turn-based match

Lobby

Invite a colleague, place your fleet, and play only outside office peak hours.

Rules

Quick rules

  • 2 players only.
  • Only 1 live match can run across Connect at a time.
  • No play during office hours.
  • A paused match resumes automatically after blocked hours.
  • One turn means one shot.
  • Sink all enemy ships to win.
