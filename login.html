Access Policy

How login now works

  • Only provisioned employee email IDs can request an OTP.
  • Email OTP must be validated before the password step is unlocked.
  • Passwords are forced to change on first login and every 90 days.

Provisioning

How accounts are managed

  • Company administrators can create accounts manually for new joins.
  • Employee roster uploads can be imported into the backend in bulk.
  • Exited employees can be deactivated or deleted from the admin side.
